Indian Festivals

Diwali

Diwali is the Hindu festival of lights, symbolizing the spiritual victory of light over darkness and good over evil.

Holi

Holi is a major festival celebrated by people across India in the month of Phalgun, according to the Hindu calendar.

Pongal

The name "Pongal" means "to boil over," symbolizing prosperity and abundance, and it is a time of joy, peace, and gratitude.
